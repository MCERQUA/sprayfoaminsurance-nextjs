Why Standard GL Isn't Enough

General Liability (GL) typically covers premises and operations—protecting you against third-party bodily injury or property damage claims. However, GL does not protect your mobile equipment once it leaves your shop.

If your proportioner is damaged in transit, or your generator is stolen from a jobsite, your GL policy leaves you exposed. This is why specialized Inland Marine coverage is critical for SPF contractors.
