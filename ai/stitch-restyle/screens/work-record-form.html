SPFA Documentation Requirements

The Spray Polyurethane Foam Alliance (SPFA) strongly recommends comprehensive daily job logging. Failure to maintain these records can severely impact your ability to defend against claims or secure warranty support from manufacturers.

  • check_circle Record ambient and substrate temperatures before application.
  • check_circle Log specific A and B side lot numbers for every drum set used.
  • check_circle Document processing temperatures and pressures at the proportioner.

Defense in Action

The Claim:

A homeowner claimed off-gassing and odor issues due to "improperly mixed foam," demanding a $80,000 full tear-out.

The Defense:

The contractor produced their daily work record showing perfect 1:1 ratio maintained, ideal 72°F ambient temps, and specific lot numbers. The manufacturer confirmed the batch was clean.
