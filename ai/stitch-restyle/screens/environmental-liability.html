The General Liability Gap

Standard General Liability (GL) policies contain an absolute pollution exclusion. They do not cover claims arising from the release, discharge, or escape of pollutants—including the essential chemicals used in spray foam insulation.

If a drum of isocyanate spills on a highway, or a mis-proportioned mix causes severe off-gassing in a client's home, your GL policy leaves you fully exposed.
